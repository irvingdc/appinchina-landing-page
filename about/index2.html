Job Opportunities

Marketing Manager

What will you do?

Your goal will be to maximize the number of monthly qualified leads coming into our company.

How you achieve this goal will be up to you, but it’s likely to include a combination of market research, content marketing, online advertising, media relations, events, partnerships, and social media.

What will you bring to our team?

  • Native-level English.
  • Bachelor’s Degree or above.
  • Experience with SEO tools such as Google Analytics, Search Console, Trends.
  • Experience with ad platforms such as Google Ads, Bing Ads.
  • Experience with social media management platforms such as Hootsuite.
  • Excellent communication skills, both spoken and written.
  • A high level of attention to detail.
  • A self-motivated, entrepreneurial mind-set and independent attitude.
  • An ability to think innovatively, move fast, and learn new skills.
  • An analytical mind and strong quantitative skills.
  • Experience in the mobile industry is preferred but not essential.

What do we offer?

  • An international, entrepreneurial environment and a team of friendly, talented colleagues to work with and learn from.
  • A comfortable office environment with a rooftop terrace, healthy food and drinks.
  • Flexible holidays and work times, so you can manage your time as you need to.
  • Generous salaries and the opportunity to receive shares in due course.
  • Pension contributions, insurance (medical, maternity, unemployment, and occupational), and housing fund payments based upon your full pre-tax salary, as well as work visas for non-Chinese staff.

How to apply?

To apply please send your CV and a brief cover letter to rich@appinchina.co.

Operations Manager

What will you do?

You will be responsible for managing the distribution of our clients’ apps across the top 30 app stores in China and driving improvements to the quality and speed of our service. You will also be responsible for setting up additional services for our clients including localization, monetization, and user acquisition.

What will you bring to our team?

  • Native-level Chinese and fluent English.
  • Bachelor’s Degree or above.
  • Strong problem-solving ability.
  • A high level of attention to detail.
  • A logical mind with an ability to continuously improve how things are done.
  • Excellent communication skills, both spoken and written.

What do we offer?
